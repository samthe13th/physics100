Welcome to the Freedbody Tool! This is a brief (1 min) tutorial to show you how it works.

Follow the tool tips in the window on the right to do the following:

Add a force to the diagram pointing DOWN.
Rotate the axis 50 degrees clockwise.
Add a second arrow pointing at 50 degrees, as shown:
Adjust the magnitudes of the forces until the net force (orange arrow) points to the right (90 degrees).
Remove a force from the diagram.
